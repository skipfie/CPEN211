r1 - key
r7 - SW0 - SW9
r9 - KEY0 - KEY3 (KEY0 to restart the loop)
r14 - link register, use to return to main thru command "MOV PC,LR"
SP, LR, PC alternate names for r13, r14, r15

main() expects the keyIndex to be stored in r0 at the time of "return"
